# Contact

BriefLoop public project site: [briefloop.ai](https://briefloop.ai)

Use these public email channels for human contact:

| Channel | Address | Use for |
|---|---|---|
| General hello | [hello@briefloop.ai](mailto:hello@briefloop.ai) | Introductory notes, product questions, and lightweight feedback |
| Contact | [contact@briefloop.ai](mailto:contact@briefloop.ai) | Partnerships, pilots, business inquiries, and contributor coordination |
| Help | [help@briefloop.ai](mailto:help@briefloop.ai) | Usage questions, setup questions, and documentation feedback |
| Security | [security@briefloop.ai](mailto:security@briefloop.ai) | Private security or vulnerability reports |

For security reports, include the affected version, reproduction steps, and the
smallest safe example that demonstrates the issue. Do not send secrets,
customer data, private reports, or non-public company material unless that is
strictly necessary and you have approval to share it.

These contact channels are human support channels only. They do not create a
service-level agreement, delivery approval, release authority, legal advice,
compliance advice, investment advice, or semantic-proof claim.
